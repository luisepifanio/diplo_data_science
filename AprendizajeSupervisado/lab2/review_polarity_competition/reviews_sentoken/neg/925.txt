Fast-paced and funny from the first frame , this film is far too good to be seen as a washed-out , muddy reproduction . And since high-speed banter is what this movie is all about , bottom-shelf sound quality like this is the last thing you should be looking for . This re-working of The Front Page is the pinnacle of the screwball comedy art . Do n't hamper your enjoyment of it by grabbing such a flawed `` copy of a copy of a copy ... . ''
