Young kids will love these adorable characters and parents will appreciate the good clean humor in this animated tale . This DVD contains 2 catchy episodes : Episode 1 - Fear of Flying , helps kids address their fears along with teaching them that making fun of others is not a good thing to do.Episode 2 - Garden Variety Problem , shows the importance of planning , sticking to your word , the importance of work and even eating healthy . A good lesson for parents too !
