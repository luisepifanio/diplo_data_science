This movie is just plain fun . It 's got a love story and it 's got mystery . It 's got a simple moral to it as Murray learns to be a sociable , caring man . Murray is at his comic best , playing up his minimist style of acting with a lot of physical comedy . The movie has a wonderful supporting ensemble of comic actors in characters that the viewer can care about . It 's something the whole family can enjoy . There are some scenes where Murray is attempting to commit suicide that might be questionable for younger kids , but adolescent kids will understand that he is testing the endless day scenario and is not really suicidal .
