Zen Master Seung Sahn was a teacher of the highest capacity . His every word , movement and action was for the benefit of everyone . This is something that is usually said by blind , fervent disciples but I must admit , it appears he had this effect on everyone he met . I showed this video to my ( non-Buddhist ) girlfriend , and she was mesmerized by his prescence and clarity . And clarity is what its all about . The video showcases Zen Master Seung Sahn 's trip through Europe and you get a glimpse of his teaching style and the Kwan Um Schools practice . The video is perfect for those people who want to tackle the first steps to understanding the question `` What are you ? `` ...
